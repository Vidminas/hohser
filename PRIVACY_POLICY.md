# Highlight or Hide Search Engine Results Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will help you understand how Highlight or Hide Search Engine Results Browser Extension uses the data of websites you visite.

## What user data the extension collects ?

When you visit one of webistes the extenion is allowed to work (you can find the list of the webistes [here](https://github.com/pistom/hohser/blob/develop/public/manifests/firefox.json) - in the `matches` key) the extension may collect following informations:

- domain name (or key phrase) you choose to highlight or hide results,
- all preferences you choose to configure the extesnion.

All the data is store only in your browser storage. The browser storage can be synchronized with accaunt you are connected in your browser ([storage.sync](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Mozilla/Add-ons/WebExtensions/API/storage/sync))

## The extension never send your private data to any server! 

The extension contains links that lead to other websites (eg. PayPal). If you click on these links the extension is not held responsible for your data and privacy protection.
